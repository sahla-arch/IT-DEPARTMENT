CURRICULAM

The B.Tech curriculum follows an 8-semester structure, progressing from foundational courses to specialized topics and practical applications.


Semesters 1 & 2:
Focus on basic sciences (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry), Humanities, and introductory computer science.

Semesters 3-4:
Deepen knowledge with core branch-specific subjects like Circuit Theory (for EEE) or Data Structures (for CSE).

Semesters 5-7:
Cover advanced, industry-relevant topics such as Machine Learning, Embedded Systems, VLSI, and more.

Semester 8:
Culminates in capstone projects, hands-on laboratory experience, flexible electives, and career preparation.

Key Features of the 2024 Scheme Industry-Aligned Content: Developed in consultation with industry experts to ensure relevance. Practical Learning: More than 40% of the course time is dedicated to hands-on experience. Emerging Technology: Includes updated modules on AI, IoT, Cloud Computing, and Data Science. Flexible Specializations: Offers numerous elective options across various engineering fields. Career Development: Integrates soft skills, aptitude training, and research methodology within the curriculum.
