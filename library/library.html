LIBRARY

LIBRARY SERVICES

The library offers a range of services to enhance the learning experience for students. These include book lending, reference assistance, and access to digital resources such as e-books and online databases. The library also provides study spaces, computer access, and printing facilities. Additionally, librarians are available to assist students with research queries and help them navigate the various resources available.

AVAILABLE BOOKS

The college library boasts a vast collection of books of various department. Students can find textbooks, reference materials, and academic journals to support their studies and research. The library is regularly updated with new arrivals to ensure that students have access to the latest information and resources in their fields of interest.

STUDY SPACE

The library provides a quiet and conducive environment for studying, with designated study areas and group discussion rooms. Students can utilize these spaces for individual study or collaborative work with peers. The library is equipped with comfortable seating, adequate lighting, and necessary amenities to facilitate effective learning and concentration.
