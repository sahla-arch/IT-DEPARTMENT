Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department

Power | Innovation | Sustainability

About the Department

The Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department focuses on producing skilled engineers in the fields of Electrical Systems, Power Electronics, Renewable Energy, and Smart Grids. With a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and hands-on learning, the department prepares students to tackle challenges in modern energy and electrical technology.

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • M.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • M.Tech in Power Systems
  • Ph.D. in Renewable Energy

Key Highlights

  • Advanced Power Electronics Labs
  • Collaborations with Energy Industries
  • Research in Renewable Energy & Smart Grids
