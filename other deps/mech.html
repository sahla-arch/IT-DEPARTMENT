Mechanical Engineering Department

Design | Innovation | Sustainability

About the Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department emphasizes strong fundamentals in design, manufacturing, thermodynamics, and robotics. With advanced laboratories and industry-oriented projects, the department prepares students to meet global challenges in automotive, aerospace, energy, and industrial engineering sectors.

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • M.Tech in Thermal Engineering
  • Ph.D. in Robotics and Automation

Key Highlights

  • Advanced CAD/CAM and Robotics Labs
  • Collaboration with Automotive and Aerospace Industries
  • Research in Renewable Energy, Mechatronics, and Smart Manufacturing
