About the Department

The Electronics and Communication Engineering Department focuses on developing professionals in the fields of Communication Systems, VLSI Design, Embedded Systems, and Signal Processing. With strong research and practical learning, the department equips students to advance modern communication and electronic technologies.

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • M.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • M.Tech in VLSI Design
  • Ph.D. in Communication Systems

Key Highlights

  • State-of-the-art VLSI and Embedded Systems Labs
  • Industry Collaborations with Telecom and Semiconductor Companies
  • Research in IoT, 5G, and Signal Processing
