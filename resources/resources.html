STUDY MATERIALS

Students can access a variety of study materials through the college library and online resources. The library offers textbooks, reference books, and academic journals across various disciplines. Additionally, the college provides access to digital libraries and educational platforms where students can find e-books, research papers, and other learning resources to support their studies.

LAB MANUALS

Lab manuals are essential resources for students in engineering courses. They provide detailed instructions for conducting experiments, safety guidelines, and theoretical background information. The college ensures that lab manuals are up-to-date and aligned with the curriculum, helping students to effectively carry out practical sessions and enhance their understanding of theoretical concepts through hands-on experience.
