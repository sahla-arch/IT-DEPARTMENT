Department of Information Technology

Achievements

The Department of IT has consistently demonstrated excellence in academics, research, and innovation. Our students and faculty regularly win accolades at national and international levels.

*Accreditation

We are accredited by reputed national bodies and adhere to the highest educational standards in curriculum and infrastructure.

History

Department of Information Technology at Government Engineering College, ldukki was established in the academic year 2000. The faculties of our department are well qualified and have rich knowledge in their subjects. Highly committed faculty members are keen on encouraging students to involve in various co-curricular and extra-curricular activities along with their academics. Also the department has a highly advanced infrastructure including high speed internet facilities, class rooms equipped with LCD projectors and digital podiums. The department labs are equipped with state of art of learning tools, advanced computing infrastructure with world class software environment and development tools which satisfy the bench mark in high end IT education. It also conducts short-term training program for both the staff and students for updating them with the latest technological trends.

Association

The IT Association fosters collaboration among students, faculty, and industry professionals through workshops, events, and seminars.

Representatives

Our association is led by dedicated student representatives who ensure active participation and engagement.

Faculty

Faculty members serve as mentors and guide the association in achieving academic and extracurricular excellence.

Secretary

The secretary coordinates various departmental activities and acts as the liaison between students and administration.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a center of excellence in the field of Information Technology, producing globally competent professionals.

Mission: To provide quality education, encourage innovation, and instill ethical values in our students.
